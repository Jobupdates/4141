Action Plan: Competency Course Portal Outreach

January to March 2025

January 2025: Data Finalization & Delhi NCR Outreach

Objective: Ensure complete readiness of the portal.

  • 1st Week: Data Finalization
    • Verify and finalize all collected data for colleges.
    • Update the portal with accurate details (batch dates, fees, course structures).
    • Test the portal thoroughly to ensure seamless functionality during demonstrations.
  • 2nd Week: Outreach in Delhi NCR
    • Engage with and onboard colleges in Delhi and NCR regions.
    • Target: Cover all identified colleges within one week.
    • Conduct in-person meetings with college representatives.
    • Deliver engaging portal demonstrations tailored to the colleges’ requirements.
    • Highlight how the portal will generate student traffic and streamline enrollments.
    • Collect feedback, address concerns, and finalize onboarding steps.

4th Week of January: Visits to Lucknow, Patna, and Varanasi

Objective: Onboard colleges in regions with fewer institutions while maintaining personalized engagement.

  • Day 1: Lucknow — Conduct a detailed presentation, demonstrate the portal, and discuss collaboration benefits.
  • Day 2: Varanasi — Meet the college representative, address queries, and finalize onboarding.
  • Day 3: Patna — Provide an in-depth walkthrough, discuss next steps, and close discussions.

Follow-Up Actions:

  • Ensure all onboarding steps are completed, including sharing access credentials and user guides.
  • List the colleges’ courses and details on the portal.
  • Conduct final verification of their profiles on the portal.

February 2025: Outreach in Kolkata & Chennai

1st & 2nd Weeks: Kolkata

Objective: Engage with the large number of colleges in Kolkata and nearby areas.

  • Group colleges by proximity to maximize daily visits.
  • Prioritize institutions offering maritime or technical courses.
  • Conduct multiple meetings daily using a pre-defined route plan.
  • Address region-specific concerns and offer tailored collaboration opportunities.

3rd & 4th Weeks: Chennai

Objective: Onboard key colleges in Chennai with focused engagement.

  • Allocate time for in-depth discussions with top-tier institutions.
  • Highlight the portal’s potential to enhance visibility and enrollment processes.
  • Provide live portal demos, followed by Q&A sessions to address concerns.
  • Work closely with colleges to finalize their profiles and courses on the portal.

March 2025: Mumbai Outreach & Final Onboarding Push

1st & 2nd Weeks: Mumbai

Objective: Onboard the large number of colleges efficiently.

  • Divide the city into clusters based on location to streamline travel and meetings.
  • Focus on maritime and technical colleges with high student intake potential.
  • Conduct one-on-one and group meetings, depending on the size and needs of each institution.
  • Resolve any last-minute onboarding queries.

Post-Visit Finalization:

  • Update all onboarded colleges’ profiles on the portal.
  • Ensure seamless integration of their courses and details.
  • Collect feedback to refine collaboration processes for future expansion.
